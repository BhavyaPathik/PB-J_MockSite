Fun History of The Duo

The peanut butter & jelly sandwich popped up in the early 1900s when someone thought, Why not glue bread together with peanuts and fruit? It blew up during WWII when soldiers preferred it over canned mystery meat. Now it's the go-to for kids, broke students, and adults who claim it's nostalgia but really just don't want to cook. Simple, sticky, and immortal PB&J is basically bread's soulmate.
